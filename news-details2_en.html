THE INTERNATIONAL CITECH CONFERENCE DEDICATED TO THE MEMORY OF ACADEMICIAN YU.I. SHOKIN HAS STARTED AT AL-FARABI KAZNU

The international scientific conference CITech, dedicated to the memory of Academician Yuri Ivanovich Shokin, has begun at the Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics of Al-Farabi Kazakh National University.

During the opening, it was emphasized that the year 2026, declared in Kazakhstan as the “Year of Digitalization and Artificial Intelligence,” makes the conference topics especially relevant.

PLENARY SESSIONS

Leading scientists from Kazakhstan, Russia, the USA, and Uzbekistan are participating in the conference. The presentations are devoted to computational mathematics, numerical methods, artificial intelligence, neural networks, and high-performance computing.

SCIENTIFIC LEGACY OF YU.I. SHOKIN

A separate section of the conference is dedicated to the scientific legacy of Academician Yuri Ivanovich Shokin—one of the founders of modern computational technologies in Russia and the CIS countries.

The CITech conference is an important international platform for the development of computational technologies and scientific cooperation.
