What is ESRI?

ESRI (Environmental Systems Research Institute) is a company founded in 1969 in the USA and is a global leader in the development of Geographic Information Systems (GIS). Its flagship product, ArcGIS, is widely used worldwide.

  • Professional solutions: widely used by government agencies and large companies.
  • ArcGIS is a complete platform: mapping, data analysis, visualization, and publishing in one solution.
  • Global presence: about 45% of the global GIS market, more than 4,000 employees, headquarters in Redlands, California.

ArcGIS Pro — a modern desktop GIS

ArcGIS Pro is ESRI software for professional work with 2D and 3D maps. It fully replaces ArcMap and offers advanced capabilities.

  • 2D, 3D, and 4D visualization: multiple maps and views in a single project.
  • Remote sensing: working with satellite and drone imagery.
  • Smart Mapping: intelligent styling and interactive design.
  • Process automation: using Python 3, ModelBuilder, and SDK.
